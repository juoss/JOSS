Know About US

We are a student group of Open Source Enthusiasts who came together for a preliminary and relevant case, Love for Coding.
JOSS plans to hold events for intoducing students with UNIX tools, Linux environment, Git Development Workflow.
We focus on Learning Algorithms and strengthening Data Structures. We also aim to conduct workshops on Programming Languages, Web development, Android Development and Hackathons in University.

  • Our Mission: Freedom, freedom of Open Source.
  • Our Expertise: Working and Mentoring for Open Source Developments.
  • Who can Join: It is open to all who loves to contribute on Open Source.
